## **HIGHLIGHTS OF PLACEMENT SEASON 2024-25** 

In the placement session 2024-25 (started from 1st December), a total of around **260 companies** from various sectors participated in the recruitment process. The total number of students registered for campus placement in the academic year 2024-25 is 1606, out of which **1001 students have** been placed. 

**62% 193 1.25 Cr** overall PPOs offered Highest placement across all Salary (CTC) percentage branches offered 

Although the total number of offers saw a marginal decline this year, students secured **diverse roles** across industries, reflecting strong recruiter confidence.
