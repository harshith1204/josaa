NIT Jalandhar was established in the year 1987 as Regional Engineering College and was later upgraded to the status of National Institute of Technology (Deemed University), on 17th October 2002 by Government of India under Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) with a mission to advance knowledge and educate students in the area of science and technology. NIT Jalandhar has 17 academic departments to impact quality education so that the students can imbibe the quality and passion to work efficiently, wisely and creatively for the betterment of humankind. The motto of NIT Jalandhar is “Learn-Lead-Serve”. The Institute offers various UG, PG courses and Research Programs in Science , Engineering and Management. The institute also promotes interdisciplinary research. NIT Jalandhar is situated in the state of Punjab which is an important center of business and financial activities and is just 350 kms from National Capital Region. It is easily accessible by Air/Rail/Road. The institute has a sprawling campus in 155 acres. It is situated in a serene environment which is conducive for learning. This institution is the nerve center where a gamut of courses are offered.

## **TRAINING & PLACEMENT CENTRE** 

T&P Centre, NIT Jalandhar conducts all recruitment related activities of the institute. With a dedicated team of Professors, Student Coordinators and staff members, T&P centre handles the placement/internship procedures. This includes initiating communication with the companies, scheduling and executing the placement processes along with coordination of recruiters and the students. Over the years, Training & Placement Centre has proved its efficiency in meeting the expectations of both the recruiters and the students.
