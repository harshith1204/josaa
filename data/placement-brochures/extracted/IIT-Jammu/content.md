Placements Highlights 2023-24 

**==> picture [460 x 277] intentionally omitted <==**

The placement season for the IIT Jammu batch of 2024 was a resounding success, with an impressive 70.8% of students securing coveted campus offers in the industry recession era. Reflecting their diverse ambitions, 13.5% of the batch chose to pursue higher education or prestigious government roles, showcasing the broad spectrum of opportunities available to them. The highest package soared to an outstanding 53 LPA, while the average package reached a remarkable 15.5 LPA, further solidifying the strength of the graduating class. 

This season also saw participation from international recruiters like Accenture Japan, I’m besides you, and Money Forward Japan, adding a global dimension to the opportunities available to students. Top Indian recruiters such as BP, Mathworks, Graviton Trading, Samsung Semiconductors, Mediatek, L&T  etc. were also key players in the recruitment process. With over 175 companies visiting the campus, students were presented with a wealth of opportunities for both full-time roles and internships. This stellar placement performance is a testament to the exceptional talent and hard work cultivated at IIT Jammu, marking yet another milestone in the institute's upward trajectory.
