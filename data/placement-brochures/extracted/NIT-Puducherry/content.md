## **About us.** 

The National Institute of Technology Puducherry is a premier technical institute in the region. NIT Puducherry was established under the 11th fiveyear plan vide order No. F-23-13/20-09-TS dated 30th October 2009 by an act of parliament (NLT act 2007) and is declared as an 'Institute of National Importance'. It is an autonomous institute that is functioning under the aegis of the Ministry of Human Resources. 

students from abroad can take admission under DASA scheme in B. Tech. 

The Institute is currently functioning at a campus of more than 250 acres with an infrastructure that emphasizes modernization. The institute provides the ambiance where creativity and new ideas flourish, producing leaders of tomorrow by imparting learning blended with excellence. 

NIT Puducherry commenced it's academics in 2010 with three engineering departments viz **Fresh More** Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics **ideas grow** and Communication Engineering, and Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the undergraduate level. NIT Puducherry attracts students from all over the country and abroad. The institute admits students into the B. ## **INNOVATION CLUB** 

**==> picture [144 x 144] intentionally omitted <==**

The Innovation Club is an interdisciplinary club that serves as a melting pot of ideas from every department. In place of solitary technical clubs, NIT Puducherry focuses all technical activities under a single composite club. By bringing together all technical activities under a single roof,the Innovation Club facilitates the free flow of ideas across disciplines without any barriers. The club presents a gateway to entrepreneurship by encouraging the commercialization of projects through an inhouse startup incubator. The Innovation Club exposes the students to the wider world of academia and industry through various industry and inter-institutional collaborations. 

At Gyanith, the institute's technical fest, the club showcases its best creations for the campus community and visitors. Among its several creations, the most notable project which was presented in Gyanith 2019 was an Electric bike, completely built inhouse by the student community. The Innovation Club ensures that the students understand the ever-changing requirements of society and build innovative solutions that address those challenges. Currently, the club is in pursuit of building an autonomous submarine that can perform tasks underwater without any human intervention. 

Every year, the best projects are selected and the students involved get to present their project at Rasthtrapathi Bhavan, New Delhi. Besides encouraging a myriad of projects, the Innovation Club also hosts several workshops and talked across varied disciplines. conducted by students, faculty or industry experts.

## **INVITATION** 

The placement office sends invitation via mail/post along with brochure and Job Announcement Form (JAF) to Companies/Organizations interested in recruiting. 

## **REGISTRATION** 

T&P cell will establish contact with the company regarding the hiring process. Eligible candidates register for the respective company/organization according to the job role and criteria. 

## **EVALUATION** 

A feasible date is allotted by the placement office with mutual consent for condu cting online exams and interviews. Pre-placement talk, if any, can be conducted as per convenience. 

## **SELECTION** 

Our placement policy is "ONE PERSON. ONE JOB". With that in mind, once the selection process is completed, the company/organization is requested to announce the final student list on the same day or as early as possible
