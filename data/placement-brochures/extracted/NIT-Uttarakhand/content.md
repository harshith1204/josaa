## **Prof. I/c & Coordinator C2P Section’s Message** 

Choosing the right college for admission to a degree program is quite an arduous task for students and their parents specifically when they are taking admission in an UG or PG degree program. ## **Career Counselling & Placement (C2P) Section** 

## **A Gateway to Corporate Success** 

The Career Counselling & Placement Section at NIT Uttarakhand is dedicated to shaping the leaders of tomorrow. C2P Section is not a bridge to employment; it’s a platform that transforms students into industry-ready professionals through personalized development, skill-building workshops, and networking with top recruiters. Our approach is comprehensive, from career counseling to final placements, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for corporate success.

## HOW TO REACH 

To reach NIT Uttarakhand, travelers can opt for multiple modes of transportation. The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, about 100 kilometers away. For those traveling by train, Rishikesh Railway Station (90 kilometers) or Haridwar Railway Station (110 kilometers) are the closest options. From these points, taxis or buses are available for the journey to the institute. Additionally, NIT Uttarakhand is accessible by road, with well-connected routes from nearby cities such as Dehradun, Rishikesh, and Haridwar. Whether by air, train, or road, visitors can reach the institute located in the serene town of Srinagar Garhwal.
