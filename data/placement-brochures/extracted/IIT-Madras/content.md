## **Saarang** 

Saarang is the **annual, non-profit, student-run** -cultural festival of IIT Madras with the ISO certification of **ISO 9001:2015 organisation** , making it the **largest** completely **student-run festival in South Asia.** 

With **80,000 attendees** from numerous colleges in South India, **5 concerts** by world-renowned artists and bands, **100+ club events** including Chroeo, Music, Fashion, it is the premier platform for Sponsorships for companies looking to create an impact on their brand in Chennai. 

Saarang is a melting pot of creativity, talent, and culture. Over the years, it has become a landmark event, attracting participants from across the country and even abroad.

## **Sangam**
