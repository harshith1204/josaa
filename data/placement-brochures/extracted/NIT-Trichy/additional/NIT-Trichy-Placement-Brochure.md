## ABOUT NITT 

Recognized as an Institute of National Importance (INI) by the Government of India, NIT Trichy is currently ranked 1   among the NITs in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).  The Institutest was also named as 'University of the Year' in 2017 by the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI). 

ABOUT DoMS Founded in 1978, the Department of Management Studies (DoMS), NIT Trichy is among the oldest B- Schools in India that is student-driven, carrying a legacy of 42 years of exemplary education. DoMS is ranked 35    among the 630 participating institutes by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)th 2020.
