## **HOD’s Message** 

**==> picture [37 x 20] intentionally omitted <==**

**“** The Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (MAE) at IIT Hyderabad is unique in India, being the only IIT with a combined department for Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering. Established in 2008 with 30 undergraduate students, MAE has grown to include 36 Faculty members, 1 Emeritus Professor, 16 staff, 229 undergraduates, 122 postgraduates, and 105 PhD students. Initially focused on Mechanical Engineering, the department expanded to include Aerospace Engineering to leverage Hyderabad's position as an aerospace research hub, collaborating extensively with industries and research laboratories such as DRDO, ISRO, Honeywell, Hyundai, MEITY, Sandvik, MRF, BHEL, and many more. Today, MAE faculty and students work together on nearly half of the industry-funded projects. Additionally, MAE has developed India's first metal 3D printed facility, led by Prof. S. Surya Kumar. There are research facilities such as a split Hopkinson pressure bar setup for different ratings built by Prof. Syed N. Khaderi, and the moment of inertia setup built by Prof. C P Vyasarayani apart from other major facilities in various fields by other faculty members.

**Dr. Ashok Pandey Head of the Department (HoD)** Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (MAE), Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad 

MAE offers a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering, a minor in Aerospace Engineering, a minor in Robotics, and honors and double major options. The curriculum emphasizes project work and hands-on learning, ensuring students grasp theoretical fundamentals and practical applications. The honors program allows undergraduates to engage in research, and collaboration with various foreign universities in Japan, Australia, Germany, USA, etc., and offers summer research internships. At the postgraduate level, MAE offers M. Tech programs in Mechanics and Design, Thermo-Fluid Engineering, Integrated Design and Manufacturing, Aerospace Engineering, and a new online M.Tech in Computational Mechanics. MAE coordinates the interdisciplinary M.Tech in Electric Vehicle Technology and contributes to other multidisciplinary programs. International collaborations include a student exchange with Hokkaido University and joint doctoral programs with Swinburne and Deakin Universities. MAE fosters an entrepreneurial spirit, with notable startups like Pure Energy, co-founded by Dr. Nishant Dongari, and Acausal Automation Pvt Ltd founded by Prof. R Prasanth Kumar for the development of automation and robotics products and services for industry and consumer applications. During the pandemic, the company and the Department played a key role in technological advancements like prototype development of automated microscopes, and product commercialization. We invite you to explore the dynamic MAE department, and we are confident you’ll find exciting opportunities.

The Department was started as Mechanical Engineering along with the inception of the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad in 2008. It was renamed Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering in 2011 to offer academic programs in Mechanical Engineering and Aerospace Engineering. Since its inception, the department has attracted a diverse group of talented individuals, including faculty, and staff, who bring valuable industry and research experience from both the private and government sectors. The department strives to impart comprehensive training in all aspects of mechanical and aerospace engineering at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels with a curriculum focused on fundamental and applied aspects considering requirements from industries and academia. To advance relevant core or interdisciplinary fields, we continuously engage with industry and Indian and foreign academic Institutes for collaborative research work with a vision to establish centers of excellence in automotive, additive manufacturing, energy, robotics, and aerospace engineering.
