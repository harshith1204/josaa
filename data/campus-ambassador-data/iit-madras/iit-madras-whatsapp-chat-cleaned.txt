01/04/26, 1:29 am - Ambassador: Attendance :
Students can complete assessments and the course, and get a grade even if the attendance requirement of 75% is not met in a given course. The attendance will be recorded as "P" (for poor attendance)
However, the following may please be noted:
Parents will be alerted if attendance is poor
P grades will result in exclusion from POR
Students with "P" attendance code in a course shall not be permitted to apply for supplementary examination in that respective course.
Students without P grades will be considered first for allotment of electives in SEAT. Those with 1 P grade will be considered only after this, and so on. In this context the window will be rolling over 1 year, so that students are encouraged to improve their attendance.
Students with no P grades will be considered first during IDDD allotment. Those with 1 P grade will be considered only after this, and so on. This rule will use all of the attendance data collected up to that point. Those with more than 3 P grades during their tenure will not be eligible for consideration for upgradation to IDDD, Dual Degree upgradation, as well as upgradation to PhD from MSc and MTech.
Those with more than 3 P grades during their tenure will not be eligible for consideration for YRF.
Students with more than 3 P grades during their tenure, will not be eligible for honours.
A student cannot claim a minor, if the student has a P grade in any course in the minor stream, except for students in the final semester.
