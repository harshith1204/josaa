## Key Data Points

### Hostels
- **15 halls total** — 12 boys, 3 girls
- **Boys 1st year:** Hall 13 (double/triple sharing)
- **Boys 2nd/3rd year:** Hall 2, 3, 5 (double/triple), Hall 12 (single/double for 3rd year)
- **Boys 4th year:** Hall 7, 9, 11 (single), Hall 1 (senior responsibility holders, single)
- **Boys PhD/PG:** Hall 8, 10 (PhD, single), Hall 14 (PG/MTech, single)
- **Girls:** Hall 4 (1st year), Hall 6 (2nd-4th year), Girls Hostel 1 (PG)
- **All halls have:** Canteen, stationery, printout, barber shop (boys)
- **Indoor games:** Table tennis, carrom, chess in every hall
- **Outdoor (some halls):** Volleyball, cricket ground, basketball

### Fee
- **Tuition/academic fee NOT provided — data gap** (PDF shared via Google Drive, not in WhatsApp export)
- **Mess + electricity:** ~Rs 4,000 per month

### Mess (Hall 12, April 2026)
- **System:** Hall-attached (each hall has its own mess)
- **Daily:** Bread, jam, butter, milk, eggs, tea, dalia, cornflakes (breakfast). Roti, rice, pickle, raita, chicken fried rice (lunch & dinner)
- **Non-veg daily:** Chicken fried rice. Butter chicken, fish curry, chilli chicken on rotation
- **Extras:** Neebu Pani/Rasna/Roohafza with lunch daily
- **Highlights:** Mon Aloo Pyaz Paratha + Butter Chicken, Tue Khasta Kachori + Egg Biryani, Wed Uttapam Sambhar + Chicken Bharra, Thu Bombay Sandwich + Chilli Chicken + Fish Finger, Fri Indori Poha Jalebi + Chicken Biryani, Sat Idli Wada + Chicken Malai Tikka, Sun Mysore Dosa + Gulab Jamun
- **Online menu:** campusmess.in

### Canteen & Restaurants
- **24hr:** DOAA Canteen
- **On-campus (9):** Mama Meow, Tadka, Chai Pani, Campus Restaurant, CCD, Starbucks, Burger Singh, Subway, Dominos
- **Delivery:** Swiggy, Zomato

### Attendance
- Not mandatory for all courses — depends on professor
- Minimum ~75% required for semester exams

### Clubs (19 total)
- **Cultural (15):** Anime Society, Book Club, Dance Club (Krazzy O've Sync), Debating Society, Design & Animation, Dramatics (Fursat Mandli), English Lit Society, FAC, Film Club, Hindi Sahitya Sabha, Humour House, Music Club, Photography Club, Quiz Club, Student Film Society
- **Technical/Sports (4):** Robotics, Aero Modeling, Skating, Motorsport

### Fests
- Udghosh (Sports, October), Antaragni (Cultural, October/November), Techkriti (Tech, March), E-Summit (Entrepreneurship, Feb/March)
- Freshers: Galaxy, Inferno (among halls)

### Sports & Gym
- Old Sports Complex: Gym, Skating Rink, Tennis, Volleyball, Basketball
- Hall gyms: Free in most halls
- Sports Complex gym: Rs 350/month (monthly/semester/yearly)

### Other
- **Laundry:** Dhobi in all halls (comes to rooms), washing machines, 1 common laundry
- **Medical:** Health Centre 24hrs, free (insurance in semester fee), ambulance available, up to Rs 1L reimbursed outside
- **Transport:** Kanpur Central Railway 18km, Chakeri Airport 20km, Jagarkati Bus Stop 15km
- **Fines:** Late registration Rs 2,000-2,500
- **Climate:** Winter <6°C, Summer >45°C (extreme)
- **Language:** Hindi (primary), English
