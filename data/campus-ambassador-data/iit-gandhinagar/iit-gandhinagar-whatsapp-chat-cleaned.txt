31/03/26, 11:10 pm - Ambassador: 80% attendance in Foundation Programme and Physical Education in 1st year and 50% attendance in 2nd year P.E are mandatory.
For all other courses attendance policies changes from course to course and completely depends on professor. (There can be 0 attendance policy or 50 or 80, whatever the professor wish)
If majority of students feel that the attendance policy is unfair (like 100 percent) they can negotiate with that specific prof.

For girls there are 2 types of room double(2 in 1) and single(1 in 1).
1st, 2nd and 3rd years gets double rooms.
4th year, Mtech and some of the PHD students gets single rooms

Boys- triple(3 in 1), Double (2 in 1), single
generally till last year 1st, 2nd years got triple and 3rd, 4th got single.
But from this year (mostly in future as well)
1st years (both Btech and Mtech)- double
2nd, 3rd - triple
4th, Mtech 2nd and some PHD - single

All this single/double/triple for specific batch is decided by the administration. For specific room students can choose by filling a hostel allocation form that is floated at the start of each academic year. This is allocated based on first come first serve basis.

For PhD students who are married/with family will get housing blocks within the campus.
31/03/26, 11:35 pm - Ambassador: Mess-
Right now there are 4 messes for the entire IITGN community. R Gouras, Mohani, Jaiswal and Bhopal. Similar to hostel allocation, mess is also allocated through a Google form based on first come first serve. This form is floated every half semester so students can choose their favorite mess every half sem.
For 1st year each mess is allocated for 2 weeks after that they can choose the mess they like through Google form (of course if they were able to fill the form quickly).
31/03/26, 11:41 pm - Ambassador: Laundry-
Common laundry is the one that took a contract at our campus. They take laundry bags twice a week. On what specific day depends on the hostel you are in.
Other than that there are 3 smart laundry machines in student lounge which can be used through online bookings - students can wash their clothes using those machines.
Other facility is 'Safety Laundry' an outlet kind of thing. You give your clothes, they say the price and number of days, you come back after certain days, pay the money and take your clothes.

01/04/26, 1:25 am - Ambassador: Fines/Punishments -
There is no fines policy unless you break some campus property.
But there is a disciplinary committee called SSAC that almost involves in everything.
If someone causes any issue that causes problems to others, spoiling campus properties, any kind of cheating etc. will lead you to that committee and based on your case you will be given social hours (you have to work the given number of hours doing charity work on campus) / semester back / year back / removing from hostel (you have to stay out of the campus) etc.
01/04/26, 1:33 am - Ambassador: Medical -
Apollo Pharmacy is available.
Ambulance services are available.
Medical center is available at Central Arcade 24/7.
Everyday there will be a gynecologist, a physician, a physiotherapist etc.
Every single day we get a mail from medical services on who is available and from what time to what time.
If the problem requires serious medical attention they will refer to outside campus hospitals and all the expenditure out there will be reimbursed later (provided you submit all the bills).
Other than this there are also mental health services with whom you can always talk about anything whenever you feel stressed/want to share something etc.

01/04/26, 1:45 am - Ambassador: Climate -
Temperature goes from almost single digit like 8/9 to 24/25 degrees in winters, and 25 to 38/40 in summers.
Very humid. Though sometimes temperatures are low it feels too hot. Likewise it will be too cold in winters as well.
01/04/26, 1:47 am - Ambassador: Language -

01/04/26, 2:06 am - Ambassador: Fests -
Amalthea - Tech fest (around 3 days) - happens in odd semester during October/November.
Blithchron - Cultural fest (3 days) - during even semester (February) - most popular fest.

Other events:
TEDx - Feb/March - one day event with several speakers.

Events conducted by clubs like StepUp, Abhinaya, Palette etc.

Sports events -
Hallabol - Jan/Feb - regular sports with new fun and challenging rules. Everyone in the IITGN community can participate, even working staff, faculty etc.
General Championship - March/April - students compete batch-wise like BTech 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th years, Masters, PhDs. Students represent their batch in several sports. Finally whichever batch has highest cumulative points wins the championship.
