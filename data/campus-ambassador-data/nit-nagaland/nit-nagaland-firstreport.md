## Key Data Points

### Hostels
- **Boys:** New Boys Hostel (1st yr, 3-share), Shillo-A (2nd yr, 3-share), Shillo-B (4th yr, 3-share)
- **Girls:** Patkai-A (PhD + 2nd yr, 3-share), Patkai-B (2nd yr, 3-share), Patkai-C (2nd yr, 3-share)
- All rooms are 3-sharing

### Fee (B.Tech, 1st Semester 2025-26)
- One-time fees: Rs 14,700 (includes Rs 3,500 refundable)
- Semester fees: Rs 72,750
- Hostel one-time: Rs 4,000
- Hostel per semester: Rs 32,000
- **Total 1st semester with hostel: ~Rs 1,59,450**
- SC/ST: Tuition exempted. Income < 1L: Full remission. Income 1-5L: 2/3rd remission.

### Mess
- Boys: Japfu (1st + 3rd yr), Sholli (2nd + PhD + 4th yr)
- Girls: Patkai, Tiyi, Dzukou
- Sunday dinner most elaborate: Chicken Bamboo Shoot (local Naga dish)

### Key Facts
- Attendance: 75% mandatory
- Climate: Cool throughout the year
- Airport: 14 km, Railway: 19 km
- No laundry facilities — students wash themselves
- No restaurants nearby — only Swiggy/Zomato delivery
- Gym beside Sholli mess
- Medical: 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM
