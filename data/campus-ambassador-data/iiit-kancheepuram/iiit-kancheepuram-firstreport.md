## Key Data Points

### Hostels
- **Boys:** Ashoka Hostel (2nd & 3rd year), Aswatha Hostel (1st & 4th year)
- **Girls:** Jasmine Hostel
- All rooms are 2-sharing
- Some blocks under construction

### Fee (B.Tech, 1st Semester 2024-25)
- One-time fees: Rs 5,000
- Semester fees: Rs 85,800 (Tuition Rs 81,000)
- Medical Insurance: Rs 600/year
- Hostel fees per semester: Rs 38,200
- **Grand total 1st semester: Rs 1,29,600**
- SC/ST/PwD: Tuition exempted for 4 years
- Income < Rs 1 lakh: Full tuition remission
- Income Rs 1-5 lakh: 2/3rd tuition remission
- Hostel is compulsory for all students

### Mess
- Akshara Mess (serves both boys and girls)

### Key Facts
- Attendance: 85% mandatory (75% with medical certificate verified by IIITDM doctor)
- Gym: Rs 500/month for everyone
- No shops inside campus
- Climate: Mostly sunny (Chennai region)
- Language: Tamil, English; mostly Telugu-speaking student population
- Transport: Bus 515B to Kandigai (1 km walk), Bus 55D from Tambaram Railway Station
- Laundry near Ultimate Store + washing machines on hostel ground floors
- Night canteen available beside hostels

### Clubs (22 total)
- **Cultural (9):** Aurora (Art), Cinematics, E-Sports, Footlights (Dance), Imagix (Photography), Literati Society, Music, Quorisity (Quiz), Theatrix (Drama)
- **Technical (13):** AUV, CS Club, EPIC, GDSC, MARS, Optica, Robotics, SAE, TAD, IPD, AMSE, IEEE, SSC

### Fests
- Savara (Cultural), Meraki, E-Summit (Tech)

### Sports
- Football, Volleyball, Carrom, Badminton, Kho-Kho, Cricket, Running/Athletics, Kabbadi
