## Key Data Points

### Location
- Kanjikode, Palakkad, Kerala - 678623

### Hostels
- **1st Year Boys:** Brindavani
- **1st Year Girls:** Tilang
- **Remaining Years:** Malhar and Saveri (combined for boys and girls)
- 4 hostels total

### Fee Structure
- B.Tech (from official PDF)
- Registration Enrollment: Rs 300
- Examination: Rs 500
- Tuition: Rs 1,00,000
- Hostel Seat Rent: Rs 6,500
- Electricity Water and SWD: Rs 1,200
- **Total: Rs 1,08,500**
- Mess fee: Rs 35,000 (separate)

### Mess
- Mess fee Rs 35,000 paid separately
- No caterer names or menu provided

### Attendance
- 85% mandatory (highest among collected colleges)

### Sports
- Outdoor: Cricket ground, Football ground, Basketball court, Volleyball court
- Indoor: Badminton courts, Table Tennis, Chess

### Canteen
- Amul (10 am - 7 pm)
- Nescafe (9 am - 12 pm)
- 3 local canteens (8 am - 12 pm)

### Laundry
- Free washing machines in every hostel
- Separate washing machine room per hostel

### Medical
- Medical insurance covering 100+ hospitals all over India
- Free of cost

### Fests
- Petrichor (major fest)

### Clubs
- Cultural: Grafica (Arts), Vadya (Music), Sync To Beat (Dance), Lifestyle Club, Yogshala (Yoga), Athletics Club
- Technical: YACC (Coding), TRC (Robotics), DAC (Data Analysis), Finance Club, Astronomy Club, Y-DYUTHI (EE)

### Elections
- 10 positions (from image): Students General Secretary x2, Academic Affairs, Media & Cultural Affairs, Hostel Affairs x2, Sports Affairs, Technical Affairs, PG Affairs, Research Affairs

### Gym
- 2 gyms, all free, both boys and girls

### Fines
- Rs 500/day for coming late after semester holiday

### Other
- **WiFi:** 5G and LAN connection
- **Shops:** New City Mart, Ezee Mart

### Transport
- Local railway: Kanjikode
- Main railway: Palakkad Junction
- Airport: Coimbatore

### Nearby
- Restaurants: Ginger, Malabar. Swiggy/Zomato available (no Instamart).
- Tourist: Malampuzha Dam, Palakkad Fort
- Malls: Under construction

### Language & Climate
- Language: Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, Tamil
- Climate: Rainy (mid May - Sep), Summer (Feb - Apr), Winter (rest, not too cold)
