## Key Data Points

### Location
- **Address:** Mahatma Gandhi Avenue, A-Zone, Bardhaman, Durgapur, West Bengal 713209

### Hostels (14 Halls)
- **Boys:** H1 Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose (4th yr), H2 Jagadish Chandra Bose (3rd yr), H3 Rabindranath Tagore (4th yr), H4 CV Raman (MTech 2nd yr), H5 Swami Vivekananda (MTech 1st yr), H9 Satyendra Nath Bose (3rd yr), H11 Meghnad Saha (1st yr), H14 Dr BR Ambedkar (2nd yr)
- **Girls:** H6 Rishi Aurobindo (Foreigners), H7 Sister Nivedita (MTech), H8 Preetilata Waddader (PhD), H10 Mother Teresa (1st yr), H13 Sarojini Naidu (2nd/3rd/4th yr)
- **International:** H12 APJ Abdul Kalam International (Foreigners boys)
- All halls named after notable Indian figures

### Fee Structure
- **Hostel fee:** Rs 19,000 per semester
- **Caution deposit:** Rs 5,000
- Source: Image OCR of fee receipt (hard to read, confirmed by ambassador)

### Attendance (Unique System)
- Below 75%: 1 grade point deduction
- Below 50%: 2 grade point deduction
- Below 25%: Fail
- This is a unique grade-point deduction system not commonly seen at other NITs

### WiFi
- Every hostel has individual WiFi
- Individual password/username per student
- Speed: 100 Mbps

### Fests
- **Arohan:** Tech fest in October
- **Cultural Fest:** January
- **Club fests:** Runit (Jan), Recrave (Oct), E-Summit (Feb), Verve (March)

### Mess Menu (Partial)
- Monday: Puri+Matar Aloo breakfast, Fish or Kadai Paneer
- Tuesday: Uttapam with Sambhar
- Wednesday: Poha+Bananas, Matar Paneer/Radhabhallavi + Chicken Do Pyaza + Gulab Jamun + Cold Drink
- Thursday: Bread Butter + Egg
- Both veg and non-veg options available

### Medical
- 24x7 facility with guidance
- Insurance card provided
- Rs 90,000 insurance policy + Rs 10,000 OPD

### Gym
- Two gyms: one girls-only (in girls hostel), one common (boys and girls)

### Canteens/Shops
- 7 outlets: Chandu Cafe, Wonder Cafe, Spice Delight, DFC, Cafeteria, Nescafe, Techno

### Sports
- Football, Cricket, Kabaddi, Badminton, Kho-Kho, Yoga, Basketball, Running, Athletics, Chess, Handball

### Elections (Gymkhana)
- President, Vice President, General Secretary, Sports Secretary, Cultural Secretary

### Fines
- Late fee payment: Rs 20/day for first 15 days, Rs 50/day after

### Laundry
- No washing machines. Self wash. Dhobi at Rs 15/cloth.

### Transport
- Railway: Durgapur Railway Station (9 km)
- Bus: City Centre Bus Station (2.3 km)
- Airport: Kazi Nazrul Islam Airport (16 km)

### Other
- Restaurants: Suvam Hotel (1.6 km), Peerless (1.5 km), Fortune (1.7 km), Citi Residency (1.6 km), Biryani by Kilo (4.6 km)
- Tourist: Durgapur Barrage, Shiv Shakti Dam, Maithon Dam (60 km), Bhatinda Waterfalls (120 km), Darjeeling (554 km)
- Kolkata attractions: Nico Park (178 km), Eco Park (182 km), Victoria Memorial (178 km)
- Malls: Junction Mall (2 km), Sentrum Mall (44 km), Galaxy Mall (46 km)
- Language: Bengali first, Hindi second
- Climate: Winter 25/13 C, Rainy 32/25 C, Summer 37/28 C
