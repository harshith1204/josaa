## Key Data Points

### Location
- Kamand, Mandi, Himachal Pradesh 175075

### Hostels
- **Boys (3):** Dashir, Sulvalsar, Surajtal
- **Girls (2):** Gauri Kund, Beaskund
- Total 972 rooms, 1398 student capacity
- 1st year: 4 per room; remaining years: 1 or 2 sharing

### Fee Structure
- Below Rs 1 lakh annual income: Rs 50,000 per semester
- Above Rs 5 lakh annual income: Rs 1,50,000 per semester
- SC/ST/PH: 100% tuition fee exemption
- Continuation fee: Rs 2,000 per semester for semester break

### Mess
- 5 messes: Alder, Peepal, Tulasi, Oak, Pine

### Attendance
- 80% mandatory

### Sports
- Volleyball, Football, Hockey, Basketball, Badminton, Swimming, Cricket

### Canteen
- 3 canteens: Monal, Parakeet, Drongo
- All timings: 11 AM to 1 AM

### Laundry
- Free laundry service
- Each hostel has 2 washing machines

### Medical
- Campus hospital available
- Medical charges included in fees

### Fests
- Exodia, Raneeti, Expecto

### Clubs
- HNT, PMC, ART GEEKS, Drama, Dance, Robotronics

### Elections

### Other
- **WiFi:** 5G Wi-Fi network on campus
- **Gym:** Different gyms for boys and girls. Morning 6-8 AM, Evening 7-10 PM. NOT free.
- **Shops:** Supermarket, Redstarter (main shops)
- **Fines:** Smoking/Drinking Rs 2,000-10,000. Ragging Rs 10,000.

### Transport
- Chandigarh Airport, then bus to ISBT Mandi
