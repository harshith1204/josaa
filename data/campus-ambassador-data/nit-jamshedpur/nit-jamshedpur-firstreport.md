## Key Data Points

### Fee Structure (B.Tech, 2025-26 — Official Notice S-26/2025)

**Tuition (per semester):**
| Category | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| General / OBC | Rs 62,500 |
| SC / ST / PH | Rs 0 (free) |

**Other Fees (per semester, all students):**
| Head | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Institute Fees (digital, exam, library, etc.) | Rs 12,500 |
| Hostel Fees (room, maintenance, electricity, water) | Rs 15,000 |
| Mess Advance (tentative) | Rs 18,000 |

**Totals:**
| Category | 1st Semester | Subsequent Semesters |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|
| General / OBC | Rs 1,25,000 | Rs 1,08,000 |
| SC / ST / PH | Rs 62,500 | Rs 45,500 |

**Fee Waivers:**
- Family income <Rs 1L: full tuition waiver
- Family income Rs 1-5L: 2/3rd tuition waiver

**One-time at admission:** Rs 17,000 (caution Rs 10,000 refundable + alumni Rs 2,000 + innovation project Rs 5,000)

### Hostels
- **Boys:** Hostel L (1st year, double sharing + PG/PhD single), Hostel I (2nd year, single), Hostels E/F/G/H (2nd year, single)
- **Girls:** Hostel A, B, C, D, Rani Laxmi Bai, Ambedkar Hall of Residence
- **Sharing details for girls hostels not provided — data gap**

### Mess
- **Advance:** Rs 18,000/semester (tentative)
- **Daily breakfast:** Bread 6 pcs, jam & butter, milk/tea, banana/egg
- **Highlights:** Mon Chicken Butter Masala, Tue Chowmein + Gulab Jamun, Wed Chicken Hyderabadi, Thu Idli Vada + Litti Chokha + Suji Halwa, Fri Samosa + Paneer Keema/Egg Curry + Chicken Biryani, Sat Dal Makhani
- **Regional specialties:** Litti Chokha (Thu snacks), Sattu Paratha (Fri breakfast)

### Canteen & Restaurants
- **On-campus:** Amba, Annapurna, Nescafe, Continental
- **Night canteens:** Available
- **Nearby:** Just Chill, Hungry Kya, The Lion Restaurant, Blue Diamond
- **Delivery:** Swiggy, Zomato available

### Fines (strict)
- Serious misconduct: Rs 20,000-30,000
- Hostel expulsion: Rs 30,000
- Ragging: ~Rs 20,000
- Minor offences: Rs 2,000
- Late registration: Rs 1,000

### Other
- **Laundry:** No washing machines in hostel, campus washing facilities available
- **Medical:** NIT Dispensary with ambulances 24/7. Nearby: Meditrina, SaveLife, ESIC hospitals
- **Transport:** Birsa Munda Airport (Ranchi), Tatanagar Railway Station, Mango Bus Stand
- **Gym:** NIT Gymkhana (free). Paid: Fitness Factory, Tiger Hill Gym, Golds Gym
- **Climate:** Tropical — scorching summers, heavy monsoons, mild winters
- **Language:** Hindi, English, Bengali, Santhali
- **Student Council:** President, VP, Gen Sec, Joint Sec, Tech/Cultural/Sports/Club Secretaries
