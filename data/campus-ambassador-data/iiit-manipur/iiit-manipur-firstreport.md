- **Ahouba** — Annual fest, 3 days
- Technical: coding contests, hackathons, project showcases
- Cultural: music, dance, stand-up, fashion shows
- Dance/singing competitions with cash prizes
- Food stalls across campus
- Popular singers and bands invited

### Other Key Facts
- **WiFi:** Jio 5G in every hostel, decent speed
- **Attendance:** 75% mandatory per subject
- **Gym:** No campus gym. External gym in front of campus, Rs 1,000/month
- **Medical:** No clinic, basic first aid only. Medical insurance provided.
- **Elections:** Once per 2 semesters, some positions reserved by year/gender
- **Fines:** ID card loss Rs 10,000. Serious indiscipline Rs 10,000.
- **Language:** English and Hindi understood everywhere (campus + locals)
- **Climate:** Moderate — 25C avg, cold winters (5-6C at night), heavy monsoon rains

### Restaurants & Shopping
- **Near campus:** KK South Indian Cafe, D4 Cafe, A3 Mart (200m)

- **Delivery:** Swiggy & Zomato till 9pm (deliver to Boys Hostel)
