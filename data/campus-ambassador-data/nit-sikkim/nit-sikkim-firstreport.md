- **Cost:** Rs 140 per day
- **Structure:** 1 boys mess (all boys including MTech/PhD), 1 girls mess (all girls). Same menu for everyone
- **Timings:** Breakfast 7-9 AM (working days), 8-10 AM (weekends). Lunch 12-2 PM. Tea 5:30-6:30 PM. Dinner 8-9:45 PM
