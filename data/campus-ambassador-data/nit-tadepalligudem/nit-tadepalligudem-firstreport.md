## Key Data Points

### Location
- **Full Name:** National Institute of Technology Andhra Pradesh (NITAP)
- **Address:** Ganesh Nagar, Tadepalligudem, West Godavari, Andhra Pradesh 534101

### Transport
- Bus Stand: 5 km from campus
- Railway Station: 7 km from campus
- Rajahmundry Airport: 50 km from campus

### Hostels

**Boys Hostels:**
1. Vamsadhara Hall of Residence -- 1st year (4 per room)
2. Nagavalli Hall of Residence -- 2nd year (2 per room)
3. Indravati Hall of Residence -- 3rd year (2 per room)
4. Sabari Hall of Residence -- 3rd year (2 per room)
5. Godavari Hall of Residence -- 4th year (1 per room)
6. Purna Hall of Residence -- 4th year (1 per room)
7. Manjeera Hall of Residence -- 4th year (1 per room)
8. Banganga Hall of Residence -- 4th year (1 per room)
9. Pranahitha Hall of Residence -- 4th year (1 per room)
10. Swarna Mukhi Hall of Residence -- PhD scholars

**Girls Hostels:**
1. Krishnaveni -- 1st year (4 per room)
2. Bhima -- 2nd year (2 per room)
3. Tungabhadra -- 3rd year (2 per room)
4. Ghata Prabha -- 4th year (1 per room)
5. Munneru -- 4th year + PhD (1 per room)

**Girls Mess:** Amrutha Mess

### Fee Structure (from image OCR)
- **Odd Semester (1st Sem):** Library Fee Rs 2,400, Exam Fee Rs 2,400, Other Fee Rs 2,400. Tuition Fee: Rs 62,500 (General, income >5L), Rs 20,833 (income 1-5L), Rs 0 (SC/ST or income <1L).
- **Even Semester:** Similar structure to Odd Semester.
- **Note:** ARB and OEB students must pay full tuition during admission and semester registration. Fee payment through Central Easy Pay.

### Hostel Fee (from image OCR, AY 2023-24)
- Hostel accommodation for 1st Year B.Tech students
- Includes: Hostel Caution Deposit, Room Charges, Establishment Charges, Mess Security Deposit
- Maintenance: Rs 140/day

### Mess Schedule
- Full 7-day schedule for both boys and girls mess available from 2 images (Amrutha Mess, official with stamps)
- Covers Breakfast, Lunch, Snacks, and Dinner for all 7 days

### Attendance
- 80% mandatory attendance

### Clubs
- Dhvani, Painting & Photography, Robotics, Career Guidance, Literature & Debate, I&E Cell, Nature Club, Brindavanam Club, Bhavisya Bharat, Shilpi

### Fests
- Odd semester: Techkriya (September/October)
- Even semester: Vulcanzy (February)

### Other
- **Restaurants:** Food Forest, Hungry Birds, Sky Garden, Kadali, Ramayya Mess, Nayudu Gari Biryani (all within 6 km)
- **Tourist:** Penugonda, Maredumilli, Antharvedi, Annavaram, Dwaraka Tirumala, Vadapalli, Perupalem Beach, Gouri Patnam
- **Malls:** GV Mall, Chennai Shopping Mall (within 3 km). Vijetha Supermarket, Vishal Mart, Reliance (within 5 km)
- **Climate:** Mostly sunny
- **Language:** Telugu mostly
- **WiFi:** Available. Airtel/Jio 5G/4G
- **Canteen:** Dokka Seethamma Canteen (9 AM - 10 PM). Shops: fast food, stationery
- **Laundry:** Dhobi available, Dhobi Ghaat (clothes washing area)
- **Elections:** No
- **Sports:** Cricket, Badminton, Football, Basketball, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Chess, Tennis, Carroms, Tug of War, Weightlifting
- **Fines:** Late registration starts Rs 500. Kettle usage Rs 10,000-20,000. Alcohol Rs 10,000-30,000 + suspension for a week
- **Gym:** Common gym, not free
- **Medical:** Dispensary within walkable distance, free of cost
